Return Policy

In order to better serve our customers, Point for ladies offers 14-day return policy on all purchases excluding underwear.

For a successful refund, purchased items must be in original condition with any tag and seal intact. Proof of purchase is required for a full refund, and if the customer fails to provide a proof of purchase, we may offer different options such as exchange instead of a refund.

Refunds will be issued to the original form of payment within 72 hours of successful return. Depending on your financial institution it may take up to 2 weeks to receive your refund.
